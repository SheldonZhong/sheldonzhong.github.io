About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago, advised by Prof. Xingbo Wu. Before joining UIC, I received my bachelor's degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).

My research interests include topics in computer systems, in particular key-value storage systems and in-memory index data structures.
