About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago, working with Xingbo Wu and Jakob Eriksson. Before joining UIC, I received my bachelor's degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2019.

My research interests include topics in computer systems, in particular key-value storage systems and in-memory index data structures.
