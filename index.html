About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Chicago, working with Xingbo Wu and Jakob Eriksson. Before joining UIC, I received my bachelor's degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2019.

My research interests include topics in computer systems, in particular key-value storage systems and in-memory index data structures.

During the summer of 2022, I worked as a database research intern at TikTok/ByteDance Infrastructure System Lab.
